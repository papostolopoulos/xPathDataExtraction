2004 Sunstar IV221B 200 Series Class C RV in San Francisco, California

Stock #159413

Although located in Canada, due to Canadian regulations, this RV is not available for sale to potential buyers in Canada.

This Volkswagen Sunstar is recognized as a front-runner for a responsive handling coach that drives like a car. Want a smaller size coach with plenty of sleeping area? Think Sunstar. There are three comfortable sleeping areas: the dinette, couch and overhead bunk -- all convert to beds.

The kitchen boasts a microwave, three burner stove, double-bowl stainless steel sink, and single door refrigerator/freezer.

The bathroom has a large wardrobe, large shower, vanity with molded sink, and a residential-height toilet with foot pedal flush.

6' 5" ceiling height!

2,000 pound tongue weight.

A 3,000 watt inverter that gives you power when you are on remote camping trips and without any power supply.

Information On Condition

57 hours on the generator!

Well maintained! Of note:

Three deep cycle batteries (Mighty Max, 12V 100Ah each) installed in Spring 2018.

A new refrigerator and rear brakes installed fall 2017, in addition to energy saving light bulbs.

A wireless back up camera for safe and accurate parallel parking was installed fall 2017.

The roof was inspected for leakage in July 2018.

The electrical rotation of the driver and passenger mirrors does not work.

Approximate RV location: San Francisco, California
